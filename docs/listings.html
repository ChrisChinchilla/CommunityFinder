Listings

Listings allow you to connect, colloborate and share information with others in the community. Listings cover a broad range of topics, from community groups, to op shops, events, or people wanting to share / barter / gift food and resources

Search Listings: Once you've centred the 'community map' on the area you are interested in, you can use the search tool on the left hand menu to search for listings in that area, once you find a listing you're interestd in, you can easily contact the member who added the listing, or access other information provided through linked websites and news / blog feeds.

Add you own Listing: Once you signup and login, you can add your own listing by simply clicking on the 'community map' at the location where you want your listing to appear, you can then manage your listing in your 'inbox'. There are already several listing categories you can use, each of which can be customised using the following features:

  • Category : Allows your listing to searched for, and be displayed when appropriate.
  • Title : The title that appears on searches / Listing
  • Description : The Description that appears on searches Listing
  • WWW : Provides a link to your, or your groups website
  • RSS : Allows your news / blog / twitter feeds to be viewed and subscribed to by members
  • Date Range : Allows 'community event' listings to be displayed in the 'community calendar' and to be automatically removed when they finish.
  • Multiple contact options : Maintains your privacy while allowing members to easily contact you whether you want to use the sites 'inbox', your own email, or even your groups websites exisiting contact form.
  • Submit to Google : Allows you to submit your listing to google so it is available on google map's and google earth's user contentt search
  • Twitter & Facebook Intergration : Automatically allows users to pass your information on to friends on online social networks
