Welcome to CommunityFinder.net

CommunityFinder.net is a collaborative, locality based, community network, that allows you to connect with services, resources and groups in your community. The site uses a locality based interface that makes it easy to see whats happening in your area. Find resources, community groups, services, and events, discuss and rate information others have added, or add information about your community that others would find useful.

Search, Browse and Find information about your community

Once you've zoomed the map in to the area your interested in, use the search bar to filter and find specific information in that area. Or just browse through listings by clicking on them on the map.

Explore and Connect with the Information you find

Once you've found a resource that you're interested in, there are a number of ways you can find out and connect to more information about that resource

  • Find out general information about a listing including its address and phone details
  • Link through to associated websites / Wikipedia articles
  • Read and Bookmark groups twitter, news and blog feeds
  • Add Comments to the listing to provide more information for others to read
  • Share Information with friends on Facebook and Twitter
